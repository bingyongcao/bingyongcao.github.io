---
title: "What belongs on the homepage"
summary: "A homepage should orient visitors quickly and route them to the right depth of information."
date: "April 12, 2026"
---

For a personal professional site, the homepage does not need to do everything.

It should do three jobs well:

1. Identify who you are.
2. Explain the kind of work you do.
3. Point readers toward deeper pages such as your blog or resume.

Anything beyond that should earn its place.

## Keep the page scannable

Most visitors will make a decision in seconds. A short introduction, clear navigation, and one or two strong calls to action are usually enough.

## Let other pages carry the detail

Use the homepage for orientation. Use the resume page for credibility. Use the blog for depth.
